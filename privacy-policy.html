1. Introduction

Epic Roofing TX ("we," "our," or "us") operates the website epicroofingtx.com. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, disclose, and protect your personal information when you visit our website or contact us for roofing services in Houston, Texas and surrounding areas.

By using our website, you agree to the terms of this Privacy Policy. If you do not agree, please do not use our website.

2. Information We Collect

We may collect the following types of information:

  • Contact Information: Name, phone number, email address, and property address — collected when you fill out our contact or inspection request forms.
  • Usage Data: IP address, browser type, pages visited, and time spent on our website — collected automatically via cookies and analytics tools.
  • Call Data: If you call us via our tracking number, we may record call duration and originating number for quality and lead tracking purposes.
  • Communication Records: Any messages or inquiries you send us through forms, email, or WhatsApp.

3. How We Use Your Information

We use the information we collect for the following purposes:

  • To respond to your roofing service inquiries and schedule free inspections
  • To connect you with a licensed roofing contractor in your area
  • To improve our website content and user experience
  • To analyze website traffic and measure marketing effectiveness
  • To send follow-up communications related to your inquiry
  • To comply with legal obligations

4. Lead Generation & Third-Party Contractors

Epic Roofing TX operates as a lead generation website. Information you submit through our contact forms or by calling our number may be shared with licensed, insured roofing contractors in Houston and the Greater Houston area who may contact you to provide roofing services.

By submitting your information on this website, you consent to being contacted by our partner roofing contractors via phone, email, or text message.

5. Cookies & Tracking Technologies

Our website uses cookies and similar tracking technologies to:

  • Remember your preferences and improve site functionality
  • Analyze website traffic through Google Analytics
  • Track call conversions through call tracking software
  • Display relevant content to returning visitors

You can control cookie settings through your browser. Disabling cookies may affect some website functionality.

6. Google Analytics

We use Google Analytics to understand how visitors use our website. Google Analytics collects anonymized data including pages visited, session duration, and geographic location. This data is processed by Google in accordance with their Privacy Policy. You can opt out of Google Analytics tracking by installing the Google Analytics Opt-Out Browser Add-on.

7. Google Maps

Our website embeds Google Maps to display our service area. Google Maps may collect data about your interaction with the map in accordance with Google's Privacy Policy.

8. Call Tracking

We use call tracking software to monitor and record calls made to our website phone number. This helps us measure the effectiveness of our marketing and ensures quality service. Call data may include call duration, originating phone number, and call recordings. By calling our number, you consent to this tracking.

9. Data Sharing & Disclosure

We do not sell your personal information to third parties for marketing purposes. We may share your information with:

  • Partner Roofing Contractors: Licensed contractors who fulfill your roofing service request
  • Service Providers: Analytics, call tracking, and form processing providers who assist us in operating our website
  • Legal Authorities: When required by law, court order, or to protect our legal rights

10. Data Security

We implement reasonable technical and organizational measures to protect your personal information from unauthorized access, disclosure, or loss. However, no internet transmission is completely secure, and we cannot guarantee absolute security of your data.

11. Data Retention

We retain your personal information for as long as necessary to fulfill the purposes described in this Privacy Policy, or as required by law. Contact form submissions and call records are typically retained for up to 24 months.

12. Your Rights

Depending on your location, you may have the following rights regarding your personal information:

  • The right to access the personal information we hold about you
  • The right to request correction of inaccurate information
  • The right to request deletion of your personal information
  • The right to opt out of marketing communications
  • The right to data portability

To exercise any of these rights, please contact us using the information below.

13. Children's Privacy

Our website is not directed at children under the age of 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children. If you believe we have inadvertently collected information from a child, please contact us immediately and we will delete it.

Our website may contain links to third-party websites. We are not responsible for the privacy practices of those websites and encourage you to review their privacy policies before submitting any personal information.
