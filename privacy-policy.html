Privacy Policy

We respect your private data. Learn how our certified local roofing company handles data collection, cookies, and consumer protection rights across Southeast Texas.

Effective Date: May 17, 2026

1. Introduction

Welcome to Epic Roofing TX ("we," "our," or "us"). We are an active, professional roofing contractor based in Houston, TX. We operate under rigorous standards for customer satisfaction and security, extending to our processing practices for user information.

This Privacy Policy defines our systems for collecting, utilizing, disclosing, and securing your personal information when you access our primary website (epicroofingtx.com), complete digital structural inspection request forms, schedule emergency leak tarping appointments, or interact with our estimators and administrative support teams.

2. Data Collection (What We Collect)

We process minimal, relevant information required to provide accurate localized roof assessments, storm damage consultation, and material installations. We collect the following categories of information:

  • Personally Identifiable Information (PII): Your name, phone number, email address, physical property address, and insurance carrier name (when provided to coordinate deductible or storm claim benefits).
  • Property Details: Physical measurements, satellite maps, structural roof pitches, drone imagery, and internal attic diagnostic details.
  • Automated Analytics Data: IP addresses, browser types, geographic regions, device profiles, referring page logs, and session behaviors captured by direct website cookies and performance trackers.

3. Cookies and Tracking Technologies

We utilize secure cookie mechanisms to optimize web navigation speeds, remember your preferences, and track the overall performance of our digital assets.

  • Google Analytics: We leverage Google Analytics to securely analyze anonymous user flows, page view patterns, and local search term metrics. This helps us ensure residents in cities like Katy, Cypress, and the Woodlands quickly find the specific weather damage sheets they need.
  • Preference Cookies: These help remember your basic contact entries if you partially fill our GAF evaluation form but schedule a callback later.
  • Control: You can configure your browser to reject all secure cookies or signal when a cookie is active, but some interactive elements of our scheduling portals may require cookies to function correctly.

4. Data Use (How We Utilize Data)

We adhere to rigorous constraints on the processing which we execute on your details. Epic Roofing TX will NEVER sell, lease, or rent customer datasets to third-party list brokers. We utilize collected metrics strictly for:

  • Scheduling physical inspections and dispatching local estimators to properties.
  • Authorizing and validating GAF golden-pledge material warranties with official manufacturing registers.
  • Processing authorized home repair installment loans with home improvement lenders.
  • Communicating with you regarding immediate storm responses, tarping schedules, or claim statuses.
  • Fulfilling localized regulatory building compliance filings and TDI windstorm certifications.

5. Third-Party Disclosures

We coordinate your details strictly with certified, trusted service partners in order to deliver premium turnkey roofing restorations. We disclose details to:

  • Authorized Lenders: Specifically to process home improvement loan programs when you select direct monthly payment options.
  • Certified Manufacturers (GAF): Sending installation specifications to authorize your 50-year material warranties.
  • Insurance Carriers: Communicating forensic aerial reports directly with your homeowner carrier to accelerate storm claim benefits (exclusively under your explicit permission and written instruction).
  • Legal Compliance: Providing data if compelled to do so by Texas courts or federal law enforcement bodies.

6. GDPR and CCPA Consumer Rights

Regardless of state boundaries, we provide clear, structured consumer data privileges to all our visitors. Depending on your home region, you retain the following rights:

  • Right to Access: You can request a complete digital copy of all personal, property, and inspection records we hold.
  • Right to Rectification: You can update or correct inaccurate telephone information or property structures in our databases.
  • Right to Erasure ("Right to be Forgotten"): You can request that we permanently delete your digital record files, provided they aren't required for active structural warranties or pending financial tax contracts.
  • Right to Opt-Out: You can completely opt-out of structural service messages or marketing calls by clicking stop, unsubscribing, or sending an opt-out email.

7. Data Security and Custody

We take modern, secure steps to safeguard client data files. All structural contact entries, uploaded hail damage images, and aerial assessment files are transmitted via secured SSL/TLS channels and stored on firewalled, access-controlled cloud networks. While we employ supreme standards, keep in mind no electronic storage method is absolute. We continuously update and secure our data pipelines.
