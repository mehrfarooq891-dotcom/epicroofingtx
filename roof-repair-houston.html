Should You Repair or Replace Your Houston Roof?

❓ When should you replace instead of repair your roof in Houston? If your roof is 15+ years old and has storm damage covering more than 30% of the surface, replacement is usually more cost-effective — and often fully covered by insurance.

👍 5 Clear Signs Your Roof Needs spot Repair

  • ✓
    Roof is Under 10-12 Years Old: Minor wind-damaged shingles on an otherwise healthy system can be targeted with localized fixes.
  • ✓
    Isolated Damage Point: Water stains are centralized around a single vent, skylight, or plumbing pipe.
  • ✓
    Under 20% Total Compromise: Physical shingle losses and microburst fractures cover only a minor portion of the roof area.
  • ✓
    Intact Underlayment Shell: Outer shingle sections flew off but the internal synthetic barrier paper is unbroken.
  • ✓
    Budget Constraints: You need immediate, reliable spot seals to preserve property value before planning full-scale upgrades.

⚠️ 5 Clear Signs Your Roof Needs Replacement

  • ✓
    Roof Surpassed 15+ Years Of Service: Asphalt fibers have expanded under thousands of Houston sun rays and lost flexibility.
  • ✓
    Over 30% Damage Coverage: Multiple storm cycles led to massive shingle lift, bare sheets, or extensive hail bruising.
  • ✓
    Chronic Interior Leakage Points: You see persistent roof leaks and water rings appearing on multiple room ceilings.
  • ✓
    Saturated Roof Decking Sheets: Moisture penetrated sublayers, causing general mold, roof bounce, and structural decay.
  • ✓
    Costly Continuous Spot Repairs: Spending $1,000 repeatedly on small fixes quickly adds up. A full rewrite is more cost-effective.
