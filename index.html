COMPROMISED ATTIC DAMPNESS & WIND HOLES

Gulf wind sheared panels and heavy summer hailstones crack physical shingles, exposing naked decking paper to mold. Don't wait for interior sheetrock stains to spot a structural leak.

01. HIGH IMPACT HAIL BRUISE

Large hail crashes into surface asphalt, fracturing inner composite fibers and knocking protective stone granules loose. UV rays can then degrade underlying defense layers.

02. UPLIFTING SEAL OVERSTRESS

High wind bursts get beneath loose edge points. The continuous suction peels open rows of underlayment, exposing attic joints to driving rains.

03. DRY ROT & ATTIC LEAKS

Unchecked moisture quickly turns underdeck sheathing damp, initiating toxic wood rot, structural beam weakness, and toxic room attic mold before visible drip lines show in the ceiling.
