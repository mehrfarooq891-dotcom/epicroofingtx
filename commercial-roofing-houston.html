Commercial Insurance Claims in Houston TX

💡 How Commercial Insurance Claims Differ: Unlike residential shingle claims, commercial claims undergo thorough structural testing. Adjusters require concrete core samples, historic wind-force metrics, dynamic water-leak testing, and engineering reviews before authorizing payouts.

Epic Roofing TX operates a specialized commercial claims department. We compile high-resolution photographic evidence packages, run certified core sampling, and coordinate multi-phase progress draws to ensure complete alignment with major adjusters.

Core Sampling

We extract physical samples to verify substrate conditions and check for hidden moisture under the membrane.

Adhesion Pull Testing

Xactimate Diagnostics

We generate transparent, line-by-line electronic scopes using standard commercial pricing indices to prevent underpayments.
