What We Check During Your Free Inspection

We don't just glance from the ladder. Our specialists execute a rigorous, certified 10-point diagnostic checklist encompassing your entire system shell.

01

Acute Hail Bruising & Fracture Scan

We search for circular impacts, identifying crushed shingle granule coatings and broken fiberglass backing mats that lead to eventual leaks.

02

Wind Uplift & Crease Inspection

We locate horizontal folding crease marks along shingle overlaps, indicating wind lift stress that broke the adhesive sealant strip.

03

Valleys Integrity & Waterproof Barrier

Active valleys handle massive water loads. We verify underlayment condition, rust, and physical wear patterns on metal valley channels.

04

Flashing Seals Around Proximal Chimneys

Broken or rotted rubber seal rings, compromised tar caulk, and rusted step flashing at wall lines are reviewed.

05

Plumbing Boot Flashing Condition

Cracked rubber around venting pipelines is a very common leak hazard. We check for rubber rotting, physical animal chewing damage, and rust.

06

Attic Ventilation Adequacy Audit

Trapped heat degrades shingles from below. We check soffit vents, exhaust vents, and ridge vents to optimize thermodynamic flow.

07

Plywood Sheathing & Rot Verification

We walk the deck structure, identifying soft, sagging, or water-soaked boards that indicate active rot or insulation failure.

08

Drip Edge Installation & Code

We check the roof edges for standard metal drip flashing, ensuring water is directed into gutters and not rotting wood fascia boards.

09

Gutter Systems Pitch & Clogging

We examine drainage lines, calculating slope, finding overflow patterns, and analyzing shingle granule sediment accumulation.

10

Fascia & Soffit Joint Inspection

We inspect perimeter wood and vinyl joins, identifying mold, rot, or animal entry holes around roof lines.

When Should You Get a Roof Inspection?

Catching minor issues early protects you from dealing with major interior drywall repairs later on. These are six essential triggers for booking your roof check:

1. After Major Storm Systems

High wind shears and hail events can bruise shingles and break waterproof seals, leaving your home vulnerable to dynamic weather leaks. Get checked prompt.

2. Annual Structural Care

Get a reliable annual roof inspection houston tx audit to find and seal minor caulking issues before the hot Texas summer degrades seals.

3. Visible Granules in Gutters

Piles of sand-like granules inside gutters mean your shingles are losing their critical UV-protective shield, speeding up decay.

4. Water Stains on Sheetrock

Any brown stains or bubbling drywall in your ceiling means a leak is active, requiring an urgent inspection to locate the entry source.

5. Before Buying or Selling

Protect your transactions. Schedule a comprehensive roof inspection before buying home houston buyers rely on to avoid buying a structure in decay.

6. Changing Insurance Terms

If updating policies, carriers require photos explaining roof age. A certified written report protects your property value and claim rights.
