Houston Roofing Price Guide & FAQs

Everything you need to know about weather claims, replacement pricing, and material upgrades.

How much is a roof replacement deductible in Houston?

Most homeowners policies in Texas carry a wind/hail deductible of 1% to 2% of the home's total insured value. On storm damage claims, Epic Roofing & Construction LLC works with you and your insurance provider to handle all line items so you only out-of-pocket the legal deductible.

Why are metal and tile roof costs higher than asphalt shingles in Houston?

Metal and tile roofs require specialized structural deck preparation, heavier sub-assemblies, and specialized expert labor. While asphalt lasts 15-25 years, standing seam metal and concrete tile regularly last over 50 years and offer superior hurricane/heat deflection, lowering utility bills.

Are there hidden costs in a Houston roof replacement quote?

Some storm chasing roofers omit rotting deck replacement (plywood), flashing re-runs, and trash disposal fees only to add them to the final bill. Epic Roofing & Construction LLC provides GAF-certified, itemized written contracts. Flat rate includes teardown, cleanup, disposal, and synthetic underlayments.

How does the material of my roof affect my Texas homeowners insurance premiums?

Installing class 4 impact-rated shingles or steel standing seam metal systems can yield up to a 20-30% discount on your Texas homeowners insurance premium! Ask our HAAG forensic inspector for a certification form upon completion.

Can I finance my roof replacement in Houston?

Yes! We offer flexible payment plans and quick-approval roof financing option terms with low monthly payments for qualifying Greater Houston homeowners. This is perfect if you want a premium standing seam upgrade or need a sudden replacement.
