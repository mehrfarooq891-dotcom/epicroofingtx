6 Signs You Need a Full Roof Replacement

Before minor moisture turns into complete roof joist mold and deep structural drywall decay, look for these warning cues:

✕

1. System Exceeded 15-20 Years

Asphalt composite layers dry crack easily under consecutive Hot Texas sun cycles.

✕

2. Over 25-30% Hail/Wind Bruising

Scattered impact bruising compromises systemic watertight shields, risking attic rot.

✕

3. Widespread Ceiling Rings

Multiple localized points of internal damp moisture suggest broad underlayment failure.

✕

4. Significant Shingle Blow-Off

Mass sections of exposed structural wood feel vulnerable to heavy coastal microbursts.

✕

5. Heavy Granule Loss in Gutters

Washed composite sand grains expose core asphalt layers directly to damaging UV rays.

✕

6. Structural Valleys Sagging

Moisture absorbed into inner framing planks creates organic decay and sagging ridges.
